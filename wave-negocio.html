What kind of offer is it?

Don't worry — we'll guide you through the rest based on your choice.

🎁
Discount or special price
The customer shows the email at your venue to get the offer. No reservation needed.
e.g. 2-for-1 drinks, 20% off menu, free dessert
🪑
Table or seat reservation
The customer reserves a specific date and time. You define how many tables are available.
e.g. romantic dinner for 2, group booking, tasting menu
🏄
Activity with slots
You offer specific sessions on certain days. The customer picks from the available slots.
e.g. kayak tour, cooking class, yoga session
⏰
Service by appointment
The customer books a specific hour during your opening times.
e.g. massage, haircut, personal shopping
Not sure? Choose Discount or special price — it's the simplest and works for most offers.
